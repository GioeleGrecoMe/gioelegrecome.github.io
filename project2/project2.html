Project Overview

This research introduces a novel method to extend parametric sound field reconstruction by modeling early reverberation through virtual sources, enabling more realistic acoustic scene reproduction for VR/AR applications.

HOM Array

4 Higher Order Microphones with 8 capsules each

Gaussian Model

Early reflections modeled as sum of Gaussian functions

Virtual Sources

Reflections localized through combinatorial optimization

Performance

-54 dB average NMSE in simulated environment

Technical Details

Signal Acquisition

Sound scenes acquired using K Higher Order Microphones (HOMs) with M capsules each

RIR Estimation

Generalized Cross Correlation (GCC) between capsules and source signal

Reflection Model

Early reflections modeled as sum of Gaussian functions: \( g(t) = a \cdot \exp \left( - \frac{(t - \tau)^2}{2 \sigma^2} \right) \)
