About Me

Transforming Signals into Solutions

I'm a passionate Signal Processing Engineer with expertise in digital signal processing, algorithm development, and system implementation. With a strong foundation in mathematics and engineering principles, I specialize in extracting meaningful information from complex signals across multiple domains.

Currently pursuing doctoral research at Politecnico di Milano, my work focuses on advanced acoustic signal processing techniques, including sound source localization, distributed sensor networks, and parametric sound field reconstruction for immersive audio applications.
