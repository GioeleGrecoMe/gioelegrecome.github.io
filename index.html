About Me

Transforming Signals into Solutions

I'm a passionate Signal Processing Engineer with expertise in digital signal processing, algorithm development, and system implementation. With a strong foundation in mathematics and engineering principles, I specialize in extracting meaningful information from complex signals.

My work spans across various domains including audio processing, biomedical signal analysis, radar systems, and communications. I thrive on solving challenging problems and implementing efficient, real-time processing solutions.

When I'm not analyzing spectrograms or optimizing filters, you can find me exploring new machine learning techniques for signal analysis or contributing to open-source DSP projects.
